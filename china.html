MARKETHUB ASIA 2019 BALI, 22-25 OCTOBER

This year we will be holding our event in Bali, one of the world’s most popular tourist destinations. Our partners at The Westin Resort Nusa Dua, Conrad Bali and Hilton Bali Resort will be hosting our guests to give them a taste of what Bali has to offer whilst allowing them to fully enjoy these amazing properties.

Soft white sandy beaches, calm Caribbean shores... this paradise is the perfect setting for the MarketHub Americas 2019. As well as showcasing our new look brand identity, we will also be explaining what else is new in the world of Hotelbeds; the world's leading B2B Bedbank.

Bali enchants its visitors with vibrant dances, colourful ceremonies, luxurious beaches, exciting nightlife and traditional arts and crafts. A truly unique destination, combining religion, culture and landscapes that is a perfect setting for the MarketHub Asia 2019.
