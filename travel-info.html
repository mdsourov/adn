Location

Located in the Indean Ocean, two miles away from Java, Bali is easily accessible by air. The city has one main international airport; Bali Ngurah Rai International Airport (also known as Denpasar International Airport) and it is located in southern Bali, 13 km south of Denpasar. It is Indonesia’s second-busiest international airport. And, once you land, the MarketHub host hotels are just a 15-20 minute away by transfer.
